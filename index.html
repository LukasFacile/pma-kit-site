FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. Undertow is software — a tool for exploring prediction market strategies. It is not a registered investment advisor. See the full Disclaimer.

Can I lose money?

Yes. Every prediction market contract settles at $0 or $1. The risk management system limits losses, but no system can guarantee profits. Only trade with money you can afford to lose.

How much does the LLM cost to run?

The system has a configurable daily LLM budget (default $5/day). Most monitoring runs with zero LLM cost. Typical daily cost during development was $2-5 total across all agents.

Is Kalshi legal?

Kalshi is regulated by the U.S. CFTC and available to eligible U.S. residents. Check Kalshi's site for your state's eligibility.

Can I add my own strategies?

Yes. Drop a Python file in ~/.pma/strategies/ or publish a package. Any class extending Strategy is auto-discovered by the agents.

What happens when a strategy loses?

That's expected. The system has hard circuit breakers: 5 consecutive losses triggers auto-demotion, 30% drawdown triggers a halt. During development testing, the system automatically caught and stopped a 14-trade losing streak.

What makes this different from a trading bot?

Trading bots execute a fixed strategy. Undertow is a self-correcting research organization. The agents argue with each other, reject bad strategies, and continuously discover new ones. The starter strategies are seeds — SAGE finds the rest.
