Four Agents. One Mission.

CONDUCTOR

Portfolio Manager

Allocates capital across strategies, enforces risk limits, promotes winners, and demotes losers. The strategic brain.

SAGE

Researcher

Autonomously discovers new edges by analyzing market data, testing hypotheses, and writing research findings. Runs 24/7.

CRITIC

QA Auditor

Pokes holes in every strategy. Detects overfitting, degradation, and sample-size issues before they cost you money.

OVERSEER

Self-Evaluator

Audits the entire system's decisions. Catches concentration risk, stale strategies, and missed opportunities.

Built for Serious Prediction Markets

Autonomous Research

SAGE doesn't wait for instructions. It analyzes market categories, finds patterns, backtests hypotheses, and proposes strategies — all on its own.

Battle-Tested Risk Management

Kelly-optimal sizing, daily loss limits, 5-loss circuit breakers, 30% drawdown halts. Rules enforced automatically, never overridden.

Full Backtesting Engine

Replay any strategy against historical data. Get ROI, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, win rate, and P&L curves before risking a dollar.

Chat With Your Agents

Ask CONDUCTOR about your portfolio. Ask SAGE what it's researching. Ask CRITIC why it rejected a strategy. Real conversations with real context.

Plugin Strategies

5 starter strategies included. Write your own in Python or install community strategies. The agents evaluate them all equally.

Browser Dashboard

Portfolio overview, agent activity feed, strategy performance, LLM cost tracking, and settings — all at localhost:8000.

$ pip install undertow-ai
$ undertow setup
$ undertow serve
